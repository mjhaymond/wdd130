Site Name

Rafting America Company

Purpose

Rafting America Company has carefully selected and created the most amazing white-water rafting tours in America. Our rafting adventures are free from the chaotic distractions of life as we guide you through some of the most beautiful natural wonders in the world. We offer the opportunity for relaxation and exhilaration as you create memories that will last a lifetime on these unforgettable guided expeditions.

Target Audience and Scenarios

Target audience: Young single adults, young married couples, families with older children, corporate retreats.

Scenario #1- A group of single collage graduates going on a celebratory graduation trip.

Scenario #2- A newly married couple going on an adventurous honeymoon.

Scenario #3- Families with older children going on an adventurous vacation.

Scenario #4- A corporate business group taking their employees on a corporate retreat.
