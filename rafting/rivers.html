The White Salmon River in Oregon

Rafting the White Salmon River is a thrill of a lifetime! Sporting a stunning 14-foot waterfall, this river is and adrenaline-pumping rafting trip you will never forget! The White Salmon River is located about 90 minutes east of the Portland area. This river cuts its way through a beautiful, forested gorge, and the water is cold from glacier runoff. The rapids are intense and plentiful while the weather is moderate and pleasant. The White Salmon River is high adventure for the whole family!

All rafting equipment and gear (lifejackets, paddles, helmets, etc.) will be provided

The Klickitat River in Washington

Rafting the Klickitat River is a spactacular day trip in a remote and beautiful canyon. The Klickitat River is locaded about 2 hours northeast of the Portland area. It is a 21 mile trip that takes the whole day to run. A well deserved lunch will be served on the bank of this beautiful river, where you can sit and rest up for the second half of the run. This river offers numerous rapids, georgous scenery, and a day full of excitement!.
