A love of adventure

Here at Rafting America we have a love of adventure! Our river trips offer a safe and
fun way to spend time with those you love in a place that you will love. Our river tours
offer plenty of "thrills" amid the relaxing "chills", which is something the whole family
can enjoy.

Come enjoy the great outdoors!

Come enjoy the wonders of nature as you float the amazing rivers of the United States.
We believe there is no better way to relax and recharge then a trip down the river. With
the Rafting America Company you are guaranteed to have a good time. Our company strives
for excellent service, giving you the opportunity to create memories to last a lifetime.

Whitewater Rafting and Kayaking

At Rafting America we only offer the best guides and equipment to our customers. Your safty
and enjoyment are our highest priority. When your equipment works well you are better able
to relax and enjoy the river and soak in the beauty that surrounds you.
